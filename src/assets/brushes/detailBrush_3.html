Description

WORK STUFF Detailing Brushes – A series of high quality detailing brushes for interior and exterior use.
It is perfect for cleaning rims and during prewash. It’s also perfect for cleaning the interior of the car. The brush handle is made of a rubber material. The bristles are made of a combination of natural and synthetic materials. The rubber handle is better for cleaning recessed areas as it reduces the risk of bruising the paintwork. The brush, to a large extent, absorbs chemicals, so you can distribute a larger amount of the product on a given element.

USE:

  • Rims
  • Prewash
  • Car interior

Size 16/24/30 mm

Main features:

  • High quality
  • Plastic ferrule
  • Very thick and delicate bristles
  • Rubber Handle
